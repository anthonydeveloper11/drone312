Why Roter?

With 85+ years of expertise, Roter is India's trusted leader in surveying and instruments, backed by the government and defence. As a 4th-generation pioneer in digital Nation-Building, we offer advanced geospatial solutions and drone technology. Our unique edge lies in integrating LiDAR with SLAM modules, coupled with IMU and GNSS, to create flawless field workflows in GIS, LIS, and DPWS. From aerial data to ground insights, we provide comprehensive, cutting-edge solutions. Specialising in OPMMS and CMS, we're your one-stop geospatial solution.

About Roter

Welcome to Roter, a beacon of innovation in the surveying and geospatial intelligence landscape. Founded in 1936, we've seamlessly transitioned from traditional surveying methods to embrace cutting-edge technologies like GPS, GIS, LIS, DPWS, and LiDAR. Our expertise isn't confined to land and mines; we're also shaping the future of infrastructure through Geomatic data capture.

As industry veterans with a rich history, Roter serves as your all-encompassing hub for surveying solutions. We're not just about years of experience; we're passionate innovators committed to setting new industry standards. From crafting top-of-the-line surveying equipment to providing unmatched after-sales service, we've got you covered.

Our latest foray into drone technology is revolutionising aerial data collection and analytics. Our team is agile, tech-savvy, and perpetually aligned with emerging trends, ensuring that we're always ahead of the curve.

Quality and customer satisfaction are our cornerstones. We offer premium surveying instruments and software solutions at competitive prices, but what truly sets us apart is our dedication to continuous innovation.

Embark on this exciting journey with us as we continue to redefine what's possible in the surveying and geospatial sectors, building on a legacy of innovation and excellence.
